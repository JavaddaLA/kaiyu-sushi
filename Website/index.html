Our Story

A Hidden Gem Worth Discovering

The Kaiyu Philosophy

Every piece of sushi at Kaiyu is a testament to the Japanese principle of kodawari — an uncompromising pursuit of perfection. We obsess over every detail, from fish sourcing to rice temperature.

Daily-Fresh Sourcing

Our fish arrives fresh every morning from trusted suppliers who share our standards. We inspect every delivery personally, because quality sushi starts long before it reaches the cutting board.

Chef-Driven Craft

Our itamae (sushi chef) brings years of traditional training and a creative spirit to every plate. Each cut is intentional, each presentation thoughtful — this is sushi as art form.

Neighborhood Heart

Kaiyu Sushi isn't just a restaurant — it's a gathering place for our Van Nuys community. Our loyal regulars, our 5-star reputation, and our intimate setting make every visit feel like coming home.
